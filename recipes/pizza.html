Pizza Recipe

Home
  • Ingredients:
    • Pizza dough
    • Tomato sauce
    • Shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Olive oil
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Salt and pepper
    • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Instructions:
    1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
    2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
    3. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
    4. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
    6. Add your choice of toppings and season with salt and pepper.
    7. Drizzle olive oil over the top for extra flavor.
    8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
    9. Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves, and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
    10. Enjoy your homemade pizza!

    Note: You can customize the recipe by adding different sauces, cheeses, or toppings to suit your taste.
