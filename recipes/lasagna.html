Lasagna Recipes

Home
  • Ingredients:
    • Lasagna noodles
    • Ground beef
    • Ricotta cheese
    • Marinara sauce
    • Shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Grated Parmesan cheese
    • Garlic
    • Onion
    • Olive oil
    • Salt and pepper
    • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Instructions:
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
    3. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, sauté until translucent.
    4. Add ground beef to the skillet, cook until browned. Season with salt and pepper.
    5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    6. Layer noodles, ricotta cheese, ground beef mixture, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
    7. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil if desired.
    8. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
    9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
    10. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!

    Note: You can customize the recipe by adding vegetables like spinach or mushrooms, or using different types of cheese.
